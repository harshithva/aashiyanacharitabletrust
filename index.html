About Aashiyana charitable trust

We Serve the Humanity

AASHIYANA CHARITABLE TRUST was established in Mangalore with the far reaching vision of Shoun Rodrigues at Jayashree Gate, Jayanagar Road, Maroli Village, Mangalore with the main objective to help the needy persons and give them a better life for the welfare of the community at large without discrimination of caste, creed, colour etc.

The charitys main objective is to assist and provide poor by constructing communtiy halls,rest houses etc.,and also by teaching of art,crafts,engineering,computer and similar skills including industrial training and job training etc.

We are also helping to maintain,to run,to administer,to develop,to improve,educational instutituions,organizations.
