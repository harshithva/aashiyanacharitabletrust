AboutAashiyana charitable trust

We Serve the Huminity

AASHIYANA CHARITABLE TRUST was established in Mangalore with the far reaching vision of Mr.Shoun Rodrigues at Jayashree Gate, Jayanagar Road, Maroli Village, Mangalore with the main objective to help the needy persons and give them a better life for the welfare of the community at large without discrimination of caste, creed, colour etc.

The Trust is registered under the Indian Trust Act 1882 at the Sub Registrar office, Mangalore vide Reg. No. MGT-4-00055-2019-20 on June 19th 2019

Objectives
I. RELIEF TO THE POOR & PUBLIC SERVICES:
• To assist and provide for the poor by constructing community halls, rest houses etc., and also by teaching of art, crafts, engineering, computer and similar skills including Industrial Training and Job Training etc
• To conduct feeding of the poor, to give food, clothing and cash grants to the poor, to grant donations for the support of the inmates of orphanages, old age homes, rescue and rehabilitation centers etc.,
• To provide relief by giving cash grants to the victims in time of natural calamities like Earthquake, Floods, Famines etc.,
• To donate towards cremation in the case of poor, deserving people.
• To provide for education and/or maintenance of poor widows, mentally retarded and physically handicapped persons.
• To adopt villages and slums for the development and for that purpose plan and execute any development projects and to enroll as members any persons willing to assist the trust.
• To do all that is necessary and incidental for the purpose of achieving the above said aims and objects.
II. EDUCATIONAL:
• To establish, to maintain, to run, to administer, to develop, to improve, educational institutions, organizations.
• To extend grant, to donate, to aid in the establishment, maintenance, improvement and extension of schools, colleges, polytechnic, engineering, medical, art and cultural, vocational, training centers, research centers, adult education centers, hostels and boarding rooms for students etc.,
• To institute studentship, scholarships, stipends, medals, prizes etc., to help and encourage deserving students and provide monetary aid to students, scholars, and teachers for pursuing their studies or education.
• To establish, maintain, improve, develop and donate for the libraries, books, book-banks related to education, culture, philosophy etc.,
• To organise, to assist in organising, to conduct, to assist in conducting lectures, seminars, symposiums, etc., on art, science, commerce and on any other subject or subjects of general and academic interest on importance to the students, lectures etc.,
III MEDICAL:
• To establish, to maintain, to run, to administer, to develop, to improve, to extend hospitals, blood banks, drug banks, clinics, dispensaries, medical laboratories, research institutes specially on medical research in modern medicines including Indian systems of medicines like Ayurvedic etc.,
• To grant, donate and to aid in the establishment, maintenance, administration, improvement of the above said institutions and to the institutions affording treatment, cure, rest, recuperation and other medical relief to the physically handicapped, the aged and the mentally retarded.
• To organise, to conduct, to assist in conducting, organising and running of free medical camps, health check up camps in rural and urban areas and popularise the use of modern techniques of treatments and popularize family planning.
• To do all that is necessary and incidental for the purpose of achieving the above said aims and objects.
IV OTHER OBJECTS OF GENERAL PUBLIC UTILITY:
• To Establish, to run and to aid free public reading rooms and libraries.
• To Establish, maintain and aid free meditation centers to which all sections of the public shall have access without any restrictions or reservations of whatsoever.
• To aid and assist by way of donations for the purpose of renovations of repairs of temples, mosques, churches, Gurudwars or any other place or places notified by the Central Government in the Official Gazette for the purpose of section 80G of the Income Tax Act to be of historic, archaeological or artistic importance or to be a place of public worship of renown throughout any State or States of India.
• To provide amenities for holding Utsavas, discourses on Philosophy and allied subjects, which encourages Culture, tradition and the way of life.
• To organise, to conduct, to assist in conducting, organising and running of free medical camps in rural areas and popularise family planning programmes.
• To establish, to run Hostels, Old age homes, etc.,. • To do all that is necessary and incidental for the purpose of achieving the above said aims and objects.
• To enable the poor changes their life situation through empowering them to address the root cause of poverty.
• To enable women gain Socio-economic and political equality through improving the Socio-cultural and economic status of women.
• To Improve the Socio-cultural economic position of the rural poor women with focus on the economically back word people through sustainable development process and integrating them with the mainstreams development policies, programmes ad institutions.
• To promote people institutions through participatory community based approve.
• To promote sustainable family based livelihood programmes in the rural and urban areas.
• To support and establish market linkage for the rural products.
• To develop Social Security Systems among the unorganized Sectors and people Institutions.
• To develop information and dissemination system by establishing computer Kiosks.
• To develop system to provide soft loans to rural poor by promoting micro finance institutions.
• To restore ecological balance with the active involvement of the poor, particularly women without burdening them further.
